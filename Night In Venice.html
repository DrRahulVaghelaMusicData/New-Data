Story behind Composition

Night In Venice

Calming And soothing Classic Style Fusion Music with adagio to grave (slower tempo) work composed for Special place called Venice . A city of mystery and beauty and having lots of real life stories from this place. Story Theme- A man who is working in family business in Venice city but he always plays cello while no one is around and one day afte being hurt by others he goes to riverside and plays cello of his own mood while being stressed wanting to relax more from his cello notes and thinking of no one is accepting his passion but then some unknown musician with harp and piano joins him to create more soulful relaxing melody and finally he feels alive by ending large key notes.
