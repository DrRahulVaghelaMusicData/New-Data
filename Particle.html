Story behind Composition

Particle

This is very simple classic piano music composed solely only with piano keys including progressive notes of keys from 1/8 to full notes in a arranged manner through adagio to grave tempo & vice versa.Theme Of the work came into vision for a kid girl who starts feeling really happy in the first snowy weather of the season & learns to play big piano with small fingers so when she practices at home , her mother told her to play simple and elegant.She just watch slow snowing out of the window and started to synchronize her rhythm of playing with nature of snow which was very simple for her but she felt happy after playing her first original music just sitting at home.The Amount of Pressures and tensions on key holds creates such type of slow effect of elongated & reverb notes.
