Story behind Composition

A Walk In A Dream

This is the regular orchestral band work with all the traditional instruments and marching percussions with drum bass.starts with the harp playing in fast to mid slow tempo then it starts to sync with violin and cello with only few major piano chords to make dreamy melody for classic orchestral lovers. so this was my first orchestral work but posted very late on plateforms.
