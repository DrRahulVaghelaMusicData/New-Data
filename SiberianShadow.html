Siberian Shadow

Winter classic is here with modern composition on top with new techniques added to in with story of man travelling to a different part of the world on research basis. Warrior drums and percussions starts in the begining because he is doind study on some kind of war histry happend in past in the region.Reverse viola and Cello half key notes gives mysterious vibrations to the musical work.Visiting siberia he makes a husky friend after finishing all research so artwork reflects the story theme of the man with yellow color express brighter shades.
