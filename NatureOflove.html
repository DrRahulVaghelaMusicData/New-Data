Story behind Composition

Nature Of Love

This Work is Very Close to me and its very special when it was created. Classical piano music showing various ranges of tempo from adagio to fast paced with simple keys using different EQ frequency in master recording with virtual panning to give your ears real time playing effect. Theme- A Girl is always jumping in new relationships & falls faster with broken hearts while she thinks its not in her control over her emotions about what she is doing but in reality people are blaming her but then she feels pain & cries alone without telling anyone.External soul looks happy but deep down she cries loud for real love that she couldn`t find. So notes are playing with unusual start which will reflect her unusual interactions in past but ends with secret vibes.
